⚠️ What our figures don't say

A model's transparency is judged by what it admits, not by what it displays.

  • Over the period, our alerts (sargassum expected) carry a false-alarm rate of about 23% in calm season: the model “cries wolf” more than it should. So we issue them at cautious confidence and flag them as such until satellite data confirms.
  • Beyond D+3, reliability decreases: the forecast becomes indicative.
  • The headline figure (100% “clean water”) holds for this regime and this period — it will be recomputed, up or down, on every update.
Method

How it's measured

🛰️ Satellite source

AFAI (Alternative Floating Algae Index) derived from Sentinel-3 OLCI via Copernicus Marine / ERDDAP, sampled 4×/day per beach.

📐 Thresholds

Aligned with NOAA SIR (Wang & Hu 2016): AFAI<0.15 clean · 0.15–0.40 moderate · ≥0.40 avoid.

🌊 Forecast model

Exponential persistence (3.5-day half-life) corrected by wind and nearby bank detection, with a confidence score per horizon.

🔒 Integrity

Each forecast is frozen at its issue date in an append-only archive, then compared to observation. No figure is back-adjustable.
