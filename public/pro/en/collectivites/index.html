Frequently asked

What towns ask us.

Does this support our sargassum response plan?

Yes. The tool complements your response: a per-beach forecast to plan resources ahead, a dated history for tracking and reporting. It does not override the official bulletin — it operationalizes it at the scale of your beaches.

How is this different from the official bulletin or public maps?

Official bulletins and public maps give a regional risk. We give a 7-day forecast beach by beach, alerts when conditions change, and a reliability figure that is published and audited — to decide where to deploy equipment tomorrow.

Is the data usable for our reports?

Yes. The history is dated and auditable per beach (export available), which documents the effort and supports transparency toward residents and funders.

Can we share it with our cleanup contractor?

Yes. The per-beach landing forecast feeds the scheduling of your contractor's crews and equipment.
