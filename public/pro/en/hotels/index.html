How it works

From satellite to your front desk, in three steps.

The satellite spots the rafts

Copernicus / ERDDAP data, AFAI index, cross-referenced with wind and currents — refreshed 4× a day.

We localize it on your beach

Our drift + persistence model turns the open ocean into a per-beach forecast, over 7 days, with a confidence level.

Your team is alerted

Daily brief + an alert when the state changes. You schedule cleanup and guest communication in advance.

Frequently asked

What hoteliers ask us.

How is this different from the free Météo-France / USF maps?

Public maps give a regional risk. We give a 7-day forecast beach by beach, alerts targeted at your waterfront, and a reliability figure published and audited every day — something no free source does.

How reliable is it, really?

We publish it on the reliability page, dated and per beach. Our reliability is validated in the French Antilles (Martinique & Guadeloupe) and shown with its sample size. Coverage is rolling out across the Caribbean; we do not claim validated accuracy on coasts we have not yet measured. Alerts are issued cautiously until the satellite confirms them — and we publish that limit too.

Which beaches and regions are covered?

Every monitored beach in Martinique and Guadeloupe, each with a dated history. Other Caribbean markets are being added, with reliability published as it is validated, market by market.

Can we embed it on our site and share it with our cleaning contractor?

Yes. The “live conditions” badge embeds on your booking site, and the daily brief can go to the front desk as well as your team or beach-cleaning contractor.
