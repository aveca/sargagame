# PR: Brand storytelling + copy overhaul — "Le Veilleur" (rollout plan)

## 1. Executive summary — the new brand story (5 bullets)

- **One mascot, one promise.** Le Veilleur is a satellite that watches the sea while you sleep and tells you each morning where the water is clean. Golden rule, never broken: *it watches the sea, never you* — a benevolent vigie, not a surveillance camera.
- **Measured, not guessed** is the spine of every surface. Verdicts come from public, auditable data (Copernicus AFAI + NOAA, cross-checked 4×/day), translated into a clear morning verdict — Baignade OK / À surveiller / Évite — never an opinion or a black box.
- **Radical honesty is the moat, not a disclaimer.** We publish our miss rate dated and by regime; calm-season alerts are explicitly low-confidence; when the satellite image is >36h old we say so (STALE) instead of faking freshness; when we're wrong, we write it down.

The story closes every day on *il y a toujours un demain à lire*.
