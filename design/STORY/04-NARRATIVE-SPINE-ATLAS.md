# 04 — Colonne vertébrale narrative + Atlas

## La colonne vertébrale (une saga, 120 surfaces)
THE SAGA: "Le Veilleur" — a satellite that wakes before you and watches the Atlantic so you don't have to. ONE THROUGH-LINE across all ~120 surfaces: "Each morning, the Watcher tells you the truth — which beach is sargassum-free — measured by satellite, never guessed." The hero is YOU (the beachgoer/hotelier/mayor planning around an invisible enemy). The villain is the surprise (sargassum + the spoiled day, the lost booking, the crisis-mode cleanup). The guide is Le Veilleur, the satellite mascot with three moods (serein/vigilant/alerte) that always mirror REAL data. The transformation is from "crossing fingers" to "confident choice." The unbreakable law is the HONESTY DOCTRINE: "Rien de magique. Mesuré, pas deviné. On se trompe parfois, on l'écrit." This is the moat AND the morality of the brand — no invented data, no fake testimonials, reliability published and audited daily.

RECURRING MOTIFS to enforce everywhere: (1) golden-hour palette = relief/safety, the opposite of panic; (2) the Veilleur mascot + its three moods bound to live status; (3) "mesuré au satellite — pas une supposition" as the trust refrain; (4) the morning ritual ("chaque matin, Le Veilleur regarde la mer pour toi"); (5) the regret anchor (~€200/$200 lost beach day) balanced by the pact ("paiement unique, pas d'abonnement"); (6) the published track-record (75% reliability, false-alarm rate disclosed) as proof, fetched live, never hardcoded.

ROLE OF EACH CLUSTER IN THE SAGA:
• ACQUISITION PAGES (homepages, SEO hubs, today/near-me/weekend, regions) = THE HOOK & the world-map: they establish satellite authority and introduce the Watcher, each tuned to a "planning horizon" (NOW / THIS WEEK / THIS SEASON) and a place.
• THE APP (Sargasses_PROD, paywalls, share cards, games) = THE DAILY CHAPTER: every tap advances the story — card reveal → verdict → 7-day teaser → the pact. The games (SargaCatch, Neptune's Fury) are the parable: you can't rake the ocean by hand (futile heroics) — only the Watcher prevents the ruined day.
• THE OFFER (PassOffer, PremiumModal, /offres/) = THE PACT: not a feature purchase but joining the watch. "One-time payment, no subscription" = a clean covenant. The regret anchor is the stakes; the 30-day guarantee + published reliability is the risk-reversal.
• THE EMAILS (welcome → drip → weekend → winback → dunning → cold) = THE FEUILLETON: a serialized morning briefing. Each email is "one honest promise kept." The 6am daily verdict IS the promise made flesh; the drip escalates value (J+3 pure value → J+7 planning power → J+14 social proof) before ever asking.
• B2B (/pro, /for-hotels, B2BModal, drip-b2b) = THE GUARDIAN FOR BUSINESSES: the same Watcher, scaled — protecting reservations (hotels), budgets (collectivités), guest trust. "We sell the track record, not just a map." Verticals are the same pact in operational clothing.
• GROWTH/SHARE (referral loop, Sargadle, Wrapped, Saga/Verdict cards, FB groups, OG images, video brief) = SHARING THE LEGEND: paid users become apostles; share cards are the daily fable spread into feeds; referral ("you both win 1 month free") is the invitation INTO the saga. Currently the weakest-told and highest-leverage cluster.
• REGIONS (MQ/GP/Florida/Punta Cana/Riviera Maya/Barbados) = PLACES IN ONE WORLD: same myth, local geography. The Watcher speaks FR/EN/ES, knows Malendure vs Bávaro vs Bathsheba. Cross-regional story ("one Watcher for the whole Caribbean coast") is untold and is the expansion narrative.
